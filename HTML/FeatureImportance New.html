Gradient Boosting Feature Importance

Correlation coefficient ranking:
Operating Income > Net Income > Operating Margin
Operating Income has the most prediction impact among all 6 fundamental factors, which aligns with the Correlation Matrix. This could be due to Operating Incomeâ€™s place in the financial statements as a simple proxy for the cash profitability of a business.
However, the ranking is not the same as the result of Correlation Matrix - high correlation coefficient doesn't necessarily main high impact on predicting target value. 


                        
from sklearn.ensemble import GradientBoostingRegressor
gb = GradientBoostingRegressor(n_estimators=100)
gb.fit(X_train, y_train)
plt.figure(figsize=(10,5))
plt.bar(range(X_train.shape[1]), gb.feature_importances_)
